## 🎯 Our Vision in Brief

Our Republic stands at a crossroads. The centralised model has reached its limits. {R3} proposes a new social contract built on four pillars:

1. **The State:** From a distant Leviathan to a guardian close to the people.
2. **The Nation:** From imposed uniformity to chosen unity.
3. **The Republic:** From proclaimed values to lived values.
4. **The Rule of Law:** From vertical control to citizen oversight.

## 🤝 How Does Contribution Work?

The manifesto is a living document, improved through collective intelligence and experience.

### The editorial process

To make collaboration accessible to citizens, elected officials, and experts alike, we do not use GitHub Pull Requests for the text itself. Instead, we use a shared online document.

**The validation cycle:**
1. **Proposal:** Anyone can suggest changes in "Suggestion Mode" on the working document.
2. **Discussion:** Proposals are debated in comments.
3. **Validation ("Rule of 3"):** A change is accepted when it receives explicit support (👍) from at least 3 members of the editorial committee.
4. **Publication:** The validated text is converted to PDF and published here.

> **Contributions are accepted in French only.** This ensures editorial coherence and traceability of debates.
